Ser OBSERVATIONS RESULTS Remarks (SAT / UNSAT)
Trials team embarked onboard at hrs on for SATs of SRGM as per approved schedule, BR 4050 and CNO 07/10. Following observed:-
PRELIMINARY CHECKS BEFORE FIRING (VERIFY THE FOLLOWING CONDITION)
01. Pressure of Hydraulic power unit (80 to 88 Bar)
02. Pressure of the brake system recuperator. (69 to 71 Bar)
03. With the loading tray in the lowered position, the accumulator pressure. (55 to 59 Bar)
04. The position of recuperator rod must be between the reference marks on the rod guard opening.
05. With the elevating mass at 0 degree positions, connect the rod 1076-17-013 to the bushing of the equilibration cylinder and jack the gun at maximum recoil and verify that the mark on the rod fixture is within the reference notches.
Ser OBSERVATIONS Recoil length Remarks (SAT / UNSAT)
FIRING TEST
The Gun shall fire 16 rounds (03 rounds at Max., & 45 degree elevations, 07 rounds for surface mode) in accordance with the following test procedures.
06. 1. 01 round fire at maximum elevation.
In maximum rate of fire. (215mm-227mm)
2. 01 round fire at 45 degree elevations.
in Maximum rate of fire. (215mm-227mm)
3. 01 round fire at maximum depression.
In Maximum rate of fire. (215mm-227mm)
Ser OBSERVATIONS REFERENCE (Where Applicable) Remarks (SAT / UNSAT)
07. 07 rounds must be fired one or two bursts at maximum rate of fire from TDs. During each burst no intermediate interruption is allowed. (114-126 round / minute)
POST FIRING CHECKS
08. The Gun has no evident damaged
09. The hydraulic system does not leak
10. The shield compartment lighting system is operating
