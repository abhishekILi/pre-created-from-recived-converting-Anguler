Ser OBSERVATIONS REMARKS
Trials team embarked onboard at hrs on for OP Checks of ECDIS as per approved schedule vide IHQ MoD (N) / DSR letter WP / 0642 dated 06 Aug 14.
Following observed:-
PHYSICAL CHECKS
1. Check the following fundamental features:-
(a) Colour vector chart presentation.
(b) Multi chart loading.
(c) Display, update and manage fully digitised and / or raster scan electronic charts as approved by International Hydrographic Organisation.
(d) Review (chart scrolling).
(e) Information on any charted element and on the chart itself including lists of lights, sailing directions etc.
(f) North Up / True Motion mode presentation.
(g) WGS-84 geodic datum.
(h) Automatic chart reset and generation of adjacent chart.
(j) Chart auto load mode.
(k) On screen help function.
2. Check following interfaces:-
(a) GPS, Gyro, Log, AIS (NMEA Ports)
(b) ARPA – 1, ARPA – 2, Echo Sounder, ECDIS Display (COM Ports)
3. Check the setting up of the Screen Configuration.
4. Check setting up of the Chart Panel and check status of update of charts of present harbour.
5. Check functioning of tools such as ERBL / EBL / VRM, Zoom etc.
6. Check setting up of Nav Safety parameters and Alarms.
7. Check setting up of the positioning system and confirm that main GPS is primary positioning system.
8. Check the functioning of the creating and plotting of user chart objects and also editing of the created charts.
9. Check the procedure used in handling of routes and schedules. Check the monitoring function on any of the plotted routes.
10. Check if the contacts of interfaced radar and AIS are painting on the ECDIS. If RIB is available, check raw picture of radar on the ECDIS Display.
11. Check the operation of the in-built battery by tripping the ECDIS power supply breaker.
12. Check ECDIS capability to preserve the record of the previous 12 hours and of the voyage track.
13. As the ECDIS ‘Operational Checks’ can be undertaken in harbour, all sensors linked to ECDIS must be running in optimum condition and interfaced with ECDIS.
Note:- The extant IMO regulations may be used for reference, viz IMO Resolution MSC. 232 (82) on performance standards of ECDIS.
Presenting Authority: Ship's Staff
Inspecting Authority: WATT (V)
