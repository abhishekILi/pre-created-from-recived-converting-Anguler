Checks
Ser No. Observation Remarks
Trials team embarked onboard at hrs on for OP Checks of Magnetic Compass as per provisionally approved schedule vide .
Following observed:-
Physical Checks
1 Check that Magnetic Compass is installed on horizontal surface.
2 Completeness of equipment as per order.
3 Check equipment installation for physical damage if any.
4 Check the mounting of all units and interconnection wiring (If any existing).
5 Check for clearance of Magnetic Compass all around. No unwanted / loose ferrous materials should be placed in the vicinity of Magnetic Compass.
6 Check the proper grounding for TMC Unit.
Functional Checks
7 Switch on 24V DC and monitor voltage at TMC unit terminal block. It should be within 15V to 40V DC.
8 Check the functioning of DC / AC dimmers for maximum and minimum illumination.
9 Check the functions of Gyro and Magnetic Compass Change Over switch Assembly (if existing).
10 Confirm that all repeater units (if exists) show the heading of Magnetic Compass, when Magnetic Compass selected from Change Over Switch
11 Slew the Magnetic Compass by taking any ferrous material nearby compass and check the reading on repeaters. All should follow magnetic heading.
Sea Checks
12 Compass swing of Magnetic Compass to be carried out and to be certified by specialist navigator.
13. Check the transit bearings of known objects and verify for correctness of reading.
14 Confirm the reading on all repeaters units, it should follow magnetic compass (if facility available)
