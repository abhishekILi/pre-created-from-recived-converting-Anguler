Checks
Ser No. Observation Remarks
Trials team embarked onboard at hrs on for OP Checks of GPS as per approved schedule vide IHQ MoD (N) / DSR letter WP / 0642 dated 06 Aug 14. Following observed:-
1 Check the antenna is fitted at a position on the ship which ensures a clear view of the satellite constellation.
2 Check for capability of acquiring position to the required accuracy, within 30 min, when there is no valid almanac data.
3 Check for capability of acquiring position to the required accuracy, within 5 min, when there is valid almanac data.
4 Check for capability of re-acquiring position to the required accuracy, within 2 min, when subjected to a power interruption of 60 sec.
5 Check for a minimum resolution of position, i.e latitude and longitude, of 0.001 minutes.
Failure Warnings and Status Indications.The equipment should provide indication for following:-
6 Check for warning of loss of position.
7 DGPS status indication of the receipt of DGPS signals and whether DGPS corrections are being applied to the indicated ship’s position.
8 Check for DGPS integrity status and alarm.
9 Check for DGPS text message display.
10 Check for indication within 5 sec if the specified “Horizontal Dilution of Precision” (HDOP) has been exceeded or a new position has not been calculated for more than 1 sec.
Miscellaneous checks.
11. Interface checks with other user AIS, ECDIS, RADAR, CTD, Trigun/MDA, Gyro, etc.
Presenting Authority: Ship’s Staff
