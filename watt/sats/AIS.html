Ser No. Observation Remarks
Trials team embarked onboard at hrs on for OP Checks of GPS as per approved schedule vide IHQ MoD (N) / DSR letter WP 0642 dated 06 Aug 14./
Following observed:-
PHYSICAL CHECKS
1 Information. Check the following information provided by the AIS:-
(a) Static.
(i) IMO number.
(ii) Call Sign and Name.
(iii) Length and beam.
(iv) Type of ship.
(v) Location of position-fixing antenna on the ship (aft of bow and port or starboard of centreline).
(b) Dynamic.
(i) Ship’s position with accuracy indication and integrity status.
(ii) Time in UTC.
(iii) Course and Speed over ground.
(iv) Heading.
(v) Navigational status (NUC, at anchor etc through manual input).
(vi) Rate of turn.
(c) Voyage Related
(i) Ship’s draught.
(ii) Hazardous Cargo (type).
(iii) Destination and ETA.
(iv) Route plan (waypoints).
(v) Short safety related messages.
2 Information Update Rates for Autonomous Mode-Check validity of different information as follows:-
(a) Static Information – Every 6 min and on request.
(b) Dynamic Information– Dependant on speed and course alteration according to the under mentioned table.
Type of Ship Reporting Interval
Ship at anchor. 03 min
Ship 0-14 knots. 12 sec
Ship 0-14 knots and changing course. 04 sec
Ship 14-23 knots. 06 sec
Ship 14-23 knots and changing course. 02 sec
Ship >23 knots 03 sec
Ship >23 knots and changing course 02 sec
(c) Voyage Related Information – Every 6 min, when data has been amended and on request.
(d) Safety-related messages on as required basis.
3 Security. Check security mechanism to detect disabling and to prevent unauthorised alteration of input or transmitted data.
4 Permissible Initialisation Period. Check for system to be operational within 02 minutes of switching on.
5 Interfaces. Check interfaces with analog and digital navigational
systems and aids like GPS, Gyro, Log, Navigational Radar(s), ECDIS,
Integrated Bridge System (IBS) and CTD / CAIO System Combat
Management System (CMS) catering for shipboard
systems of different origins / manufactures and vintages.
6 Check AIS for “Receive Only mode information when own transmission is switched off.
