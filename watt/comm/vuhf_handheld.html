SNo OBSERVATIONS REMARKS
Trial team reported onboard for Op Checks of VHF Hand held set at hrs. Checks and trials carried out IAW CNO 07/10, NHQ/DNS letter CM/0301/OPS dated 31 Jul 24 and HQENC/SOC(C) letter CM/3070/29A dated 31 Jul 24 are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs:-
(A) Physical checks:-
1. Check the set is supplied with all accessories (Battery pack, battery charger, antenna, water proof cover, hands free kit etc).
2. Check all the buttons, knobs of radio are physically okay.
3. Check battery status of set prior starting functional checks.
(B) Functional Checks:-
1.Powering On: Check whether set is powered ON by rotating (power/volume) knob.
2.Signal strength Indicator: Check the set for signal strength bar or digital read out (as applicable).
3.Battery Status: Check whether the battery status is visible on display.
4.Volume Adjustment: Check the set for volume adjustment by rotating Power/ Volume button (as applicable).
5.Selecting channel: Check by rotating the channel switch knob for changing the channels. Check display whether selected channel is displayed on screen.
6.GPS: Check set for inbuilt GPS Rx & its operations. (if applicable).
7.Bluetooth: Check set for integrated Bluetooth & its operations.
8.Making Calls: Check communication on channels by pressing PTT button on at least 10 random frequency with in VHF (136-174 MHz) or UHF (400-470 MHz) as per the declared capability of set.
9.Receiving Calls: Set desired channels for receiving the call and check for reception of call. Reception should be check on at least 10 random frequency with in VHF (136 – 174 MHz) or UHF (400-470 MHz) as per the declared capability of set.
10.Emergency SOS/ SIREN: Check generation of SOS/ SIREN in set by pressing Emergency Alarm Key/ button (if applicable).
11.VOX: Check optimum functioning of set with VOX or similar system provided for hands free operations.
