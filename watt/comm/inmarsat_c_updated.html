Trial team reported onboard for Op Checks of INMARSAT 'C' at hrs. Checks and trials carried out IAW NHQ approved schedule, Communication Acquaint Note 03/24 (NHQ/DNS Letter CM/0301/OPS dated 15 Apr 24) and CNO. 07/10 are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs: -

Ser Checks Observations Remarks
A Visual Checks
(i) The condition of all the required interconnecting cables between units / racks of the system.
(ii) The condition of the power supply, earthing of the units and the overall air conditioning in the compartment.
(iii) Stability of the power supply to the system.
(iv) Check IMO compliance / certification for the equipment.
B Visual Checks
(i) Fitment of Antenna unit fitted on (Model: S.No. ).
(ii) Power supply Unit (Model: S.No. ).
(iii) Trans-receiver (Model: S.No. ).
(iv) Processor Unit
(v) Printer (Model: S.No. ).
(vi) All sub – assemblies are fixed properly.
(vii) Securing of connecting cables.
(viii) Switching over from main power supply to uninterrupted power supply.
C Installation Completeness
(i) Check the mounting of all units and interconnection wiring as per installation drawing.
(ii) Check RF grounding of all individual units.
D Electrical Supply Checks
(i) Power Supply 110 / 220V Requisite AC input is available and measured within tolerance level of ±10 %.
(ii) The output of Power Supply Unit Measured at 24–28 V DC.
E Functional Checks
(i) Switch on main power supply and check indication and SAT C monitor (terminal).
(ii) Check and verify key and menu function.
(iii) Check present position data available.
(iv) Check facility to disable GPS feed.
(v) Check and verify system self-tests.
(vi) Check login normal.
(vii) Verify operation using PV test.
(viii) Check operation by loopback test.
(ix) Check operation with any shore address.
(x) Check message printouts.
(xi) Check EGC message reception.
(xii) Check the operation in DC power.
