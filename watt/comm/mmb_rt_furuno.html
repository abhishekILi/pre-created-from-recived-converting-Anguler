Basic Information
Checks
Ser No. Checks Observation Remarks
1 Mechanical and Electrical Checks
1.1 Fitment of TX/RX unit in Bridge.
1.2 Fitment of Antenna on
1.3 Installation check of the VHF MMB set Furuno FM 8900S
1.4 Completeness of the equipment as per scope of supply.
1.5 Check equipment installation for damage if any.
1.6 Check the mounting of all units and interconnection wiring as per installation drawing.
1.7 Cable marking as per acceptable standard.
1.8 Check that units can be easily opened up and accessed for carrying out repairs/ maintenance.
1.9 VHF antenna should be separated at least 2 -3 mtrs from transmitting antenna and should be placed as high as possible.
1.10 Check all bonding/ Grounding straps have been connected properly.
2 Functional Checks
2.1 Switch on ships main supply 115/ 230 V AC to power supply to PSU of Furuno FM 8900S MMB set and check it should be within the tolerance of 10%.
2.2 Switch on Furuno FM 8900S MMB set and measure its output, it should be within the tolerance of 10%.
2.3 Turn on VHF set and measure the output voltage of Furuno FM 8900S, should be 13.5 V DC + 1 V.
2.4 Check function of all controls and menu as per operators manual and confirm GPS input fed into the set.
2.5 Check switching ON between Telephony and DSC screens by pressing Tel/DSC button.
2.6 Confirm whether the own ships MMSI number is programmed or not by pressing SHIFT+FUNC+DSC. Own ships ID number will now be displayed.
2.7 Select any channel from 01-28 & 60-88 except Ch 70, 75 & 77. Press PTT, Tx legend should light
2.8 Check the power O/P with dummy load it should be within limit or it should be less than 10-20% of rated power.
2.9 Internal functional checks – carryout self test and make sure all results shown as normal.
2.10 Frequency – Check the frequency using counter to make sure deleted frequency is within a range of + 1.5 KHz.
2.11 Tx Power – connects a PWR meter and measure the power at 156 MHz band. Make sure it is within to designated range. Forward power uses 20W and below 25W. Power Reduction USER 0.6W and below 01W.(Reflection Below 1W)
2.12 DSC Comm – carryout test call (with both No. 1 and No. 2) and Check interfacing with DMR 16A and check recording.
