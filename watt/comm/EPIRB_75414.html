(C) Functional Checks
Ser Observations Remarks
1. Push and hold switch in TEST position for 15 seconds. Keep hand and other objects away from the antenna. A successful self-test will result in the Test passes after one signal flash only.
2. Release the switch and put the EPIRB back into the bracket.
3. Press the button for 2 seconds and then release. The Green LED will flash once to confirm the short self-test has started.
4. A successful self-test result is indicated by the flashing of the white strobe lights. On completion of self-test the EPIRB will switch off automatically. A self-test failure will result in NO high intensity strobe flash and the red LED will flash once.
