Ser OBSERVATIONS REMARKS
Trial team reported onboard for Op Checks of SART at SART at hrs The trials carried were out i.a.w. IHQ MoD(N) letter CM/0301/OPS dated 15 Apr 24 approved schedule and CNO. 07/10 are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs :-
(A) Preliminary Checks
1. Set fixed on
Set calibrated and valid upto
Set free from crack and damage
2. Ensure SART Installation.
3. Check IMO Compliance / certification for the equipment.
4. Ensure X band marine radar / AIS is operational and power supply to equipment is available.
(B) Visual Checks
1. Check the battery expiry date of SART and correct marking.
2. Check proper securing and installation of SART.
3. Check availability and fitment on mounting pole.
(C) Functional Checks for RADAR based SART
1. Locate the SART within the line of sight of operating approved marine radar.
2. Set X band radar in transmission and at 12 NM range.
3. Hold the switch on the SART in the “TEST” POSITION. This should result in a transmission consisting 12 sweeps through the range 9.2 GHz to 9.5 GHz on an X band radar.
4. Put switch to “OFF” position after completing the test.
(D) Functional Checks for AIS Based SART
1. Take SART outside and hold it under a clear view of the sky. This will maximize the speed of the GNSS position fix.
2. Locate the SART within the line of sight of operating AIS.
3. Hold “TEST” button for over 03 seconds. The Indicator will flash Green LED or Orange LED.
4. SART complete transmission and light off after long Green color flash automatically.
5. The SART position should be depicted on AIS / radar & ECDIS display as AIS contact.
6. Hold “TEST” button for over 03 seconds to initiate power off. Keep holding TEST until the ON LED stops flashing – the SART is then deactivated.
