Ser Checks /Observations Remarks
Trial team reported onboard for Op Checks of VHF(DSC) MMB at hrs. The trials carried were out i.a.w. NHQ/DNS approved scheduled Communication Acquaint 03/2024 (CM/0301/OPS dated 15 Apr 24) and HQENC/CCO Fax CM/3070/41 dated 16 Apr 24, and CNO. 07/10 are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs :-
(A) Preliminary Checks
1.The Condition of all the required interconnecting cables between units/ racks of the system.
2.The condition of the power supply, earthing of the units and the overall air conditioning in the compartment.
3.Stability of the power supply to the system.
4.Check IMO compliance / certification for the equipment.
(B) Visual Checks
1.Fitment of Tx/Rx uniton Bridge / Flag Bridge.
2.Fitment of antenna on and antenna connection to Tx/Rx.
3.Grounding of Tx/Rx and securing of system.
4.Securing of sub-assemblies and connecting cables.
5.Power supply to Tx/Rx.
6.Switching over from main power supply to uninterrupted power supply.
(C) Functional Checks
1.Switch on Main Power Supply & Check indication on Control unit.
2.Check for any fault/ alarm during starting of set.
3.Check and verify key function, volume controls, menu functions.
4.Check position data from GPS.
5.Check and verify DSC operation by transmitting / receiving acknowledgement to / from shore / ship station.
6.Check RT operation, transmission and reception on various frequencies with onboard available VHF set.
7.Check communication with other units (eg. Navy Control).
8.Verify the Peak Transmitting Power (25W) and Low Power Settinga(1W).
9.Check the operation in DC/Backup Power Supply after switching off main power supply.
10.Check interfacing with DMR 16A and check recording.
