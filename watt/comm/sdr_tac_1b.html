9. Operational Checks of SDR TAC wave forms:
9.1VUHF FM - Waveform CH 1, 2 & 3 (with default configuration).
Procedure:
(a) Ensure that proper RF cables are connected with the attenuator.
(b) SDR-TAC unit is configured for functional voice testing.
(c) Select FM waveform in channel 1 with any pre-set configuration.
(d) Set the frequency 125/ 226/361/475 MHz and Low power mode on both the channels.
(e) Set the volume level to High on the display.
(f) Communicate back to back by pressing handset PTT. Verify the voice strength & Quality
(g) Repeat tests of frequencies 125/ 226/361/475 MHz on other channels 2, 3
(h) Power on RCU & configure the RCU to control CH 1 with FM waveform.
(j) Communicate back to back by pressing handset PTT on RCU & Channel 2 of Radio, Verify the voice strength & Quality on frequencies 125/ 226/361/475 MHz
