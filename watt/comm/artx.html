Checks
Trial team embarked onboard for Op checks of HF Tx/R ATRX 1000 at hrs. The trials carried out IAW IHQ MoD(N)/DNS approved schedule are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs
Ser Checks Observations Remarks (SAT / UNSAT)
7.1 Visual Checks.
(i) Cabinets & Front panels of the various units of the system do not show any imperfections, abrasions, shock damages and units are correctly mounted.
(ii)No physical damages to electrical parts.
(iii)All the LRUs are placed in the racks correctly in the respective slots and connections are given properly.
(iv)All units and racks are secured properly.
(v)Fixing and locking nuts/ screws are tightened.
(vi)All fuses are of correct rating and fuse setting matches with input power supply.
(vii)Checks of front panel controls (a) Switches (b) Power ON LEDs.
(viii)All switches and controls operate freely.
(ix)Check availability of stabilized power supply to the system.
7.2 Bite Check.
(i)Select "Remote" & "Start" from "Main Menu" of Radio set for Bite check.
(ii)Check complete Bite test status in "Admin/BITE" from RCU i.e. HFIU, Radio, Modem, Power Amplifier, ATU and Power Supply Unit.
7.3 Voice Communication Check.
(i)Check voice test using AM mode on 3,16 and 26 MHz
(ii)Check voice test in SSB Mode:
(a) LSB Mode
(b) USB Mode
7.4 Data Communication Checks.
(i)Data checks using Narrow band on 3 MHz:
(a) USB
(b) LSB
(c) ISB
(ii)Data checks using Wide Band on 24 MHz
(a) USB
(b) LSB
(iii)Data checks using FSK Mode.
7.5 Remote Operations Checks (RCUs)
(i)Check Local/ Remote selection.
(ii)Check Remote selection of Modes of operation.(USB, LSB, ISB, CW and FSK)
(iii)Check Remote setting of Frequencies (12, 16 and 17 MHz).
(iv)Check Remote setting of Power output.(10W, 100W, 250W, 500W and 1KW).
