Checks
22 Trial team embarked onboard for Ops Check of DMR 16A at hrs The trials carried out IAW IHQ, MoD(N)/DNS approved schedule CM/3214 date 12 May 17 and CNO. 07/10 are enumerated in succeeding paragraphs
Ser Checks Observations Remarks
1. Physical Checks/Checks Prior Switching ON.
(a) Check all accessories as per supply voucher
(b) Fitment of equipment on shock mount in compartment
(c) Grounding connected to GND point near aft of the DMR 16A
(d) Securing of connecting cable
(e) Check AC mains supply 200V - 260V, 50 Hz
(f) Voltage difference between earthing points of the compartment and the ground point of AC main socket, should be almost zero.
2. Switch on Tests.
(a) Switch ON DMR-16A by pressing power ON/OFF button.
(b) Check function of front panel, switches, LED and functional keys.
(c) Check operational function of touch screen.
(d) Check operational function of USB Key Board and Mouse.
(e) Check outlet fans are working fine.
3. Checks Connecting Audio Signals to DMR 16A
(a) Check the input audio signal(s) routed through shielded co-axial cable to type RG 48(preferable) between source to DMR 16A.
(b) Check the audio signals, to be recorded, using Multimeter/Oscilloscope audio value should be between -9V to +9V.
(c) The input audio signal should be connected to any channel under "Analog Input" via BNC connectors at the rear of the equipment.
4. Recording and Live Monitoring of Signals in DMR-16A
(a) DMR 16A has been interfaced with PAE 3060, EK 896, HF Tx/Rx MBR T 5022/6222/ FM8900s and CCS MK II///ACCS Connecting to ITU Number for recording of Helo communication.
(b) Bridge Kastan interfaced for recording of Bridge communication (CON 1 and CON 2).
(c) Check audio input wave (bar) on screen while recording each channel.
(d) Check functioning of all 16 channels by recording through each channel and playing of recorded input audio into the built-in speaker.
(e) Insert audio jack plug of the headphone into the AUDIO port and listen to the audio. This time built-in speaker is cut-off.
