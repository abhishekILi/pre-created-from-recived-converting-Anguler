2. Outside Pressure Hull
Ser Check Item Yes/No Remarks
1(a) Loose metallic items inside casing properly secured
2(b) Cables running inside casing properly routed through conduits

3. Communication System / Radar
Ser Check Item Yes/No Remarks
1(a) RF paint on communication antenna in good condition
2(b) RF paint on radar antenna in good condition
3(c) Any RF leakage at wave-guide joints outside radar mast
4(d) Degradation of VSWR value vis-a-vis previous HATS and SATS measurements
5(e) All gaskets of RF cabinets in good condition
6(f) Connectors / wave-guide junctions tight and properly preserved
7(g) Wave-guides and antenna feeder cables in good physical condition
4. Inside Pressure Hull
Ser Check Item Yes/No Remarks
1(a) Equipment / machinery above 30V provided with proper grounding
2(b) Copper straps provided for steel cabinets/foundations grounding
3(c) Aluminum alloy straps provided for aluminum cabinets/foundations
4(d) Braided Type IV ground straps for equipment on shock mounts
5(e) Bond straps free from corrosion, breakage and loose contacts
6(f) Bond resistance within limits (less than 10 milli ohms)
7(g) Portable equipment with three-core cables grounded using third pin
8(h) Covers / panels of electronic equipment secured
9(j) If wooden/plastic platforms used, grounding provision provided for fitted equipment
10(k) Ethernet / LAN cables are STP and not UTP
11(l) Slip rings, brushes and brush tension proper in DC motors
12(m) Power line filters for Radio/Radar rooms properly bonded to hull
13(n) Metallic doors of Cat A compartments make proper all-around contact with frame
14(p) Pigtail bonds used for grounding of cable screens are in good condition
15(q) 360 degree cable shield termination inside connectors maintained in good condition
16(r) Cable shields / pipelines / ventilation ducts penetrating radio room bulkhead grounded at penetration
17(s) In SSK submarines, power supply lines from suppressor filters to radio room bulkhead routed in flexible metal conduits
18(t) HF Rx feeder cables routed through narrow band pass filters mounted on radio room bulkhead
19(u) Feeder cable routing and bonding condition satisfactory
