18. Emergency Supply System
(a) Trial Details
(b) Test Equipment Used
(c) Operational Checks
SerChecksRemarks
(i) Carry out visual inspection checks as follows:
  1. No physical damage on emergency flexible cables.
  2. Check continuity and insulation of cables at random (15% of total cables to be checked).
  3. All rubber lugs at cable ends are intact.
  4. All identification markings provided on lugs are in order.
  5. Identification marking of Emergency Switch Connection Box (ESCB) is provided for each phase near the terminal of each phase.
  6. Availability of key in securing clip provided on the box.
  7. Provisioning of emergency connection terminals on Switchboard section/ EDCs.
(ii) Carry out visual inspection of the permanent verticals.
(iii) Carry out visual inspection of the permanent horizontals.
(iv) Carry out visual inspection of through bulkhead connections and deck tubes if any.
(v) Check the functionality of emergency portable transformers along with emergency link box and supply distribution board.
(vi) Check for availability of layout diagrams and ship staff alacrity and awareness in laying emergency cables as per diagrams.
(vii) Check the insulation and continuity at both ends after laying out cable from service to source.
(viii) Post insulation checks, connect up the cables at both ends and switch on the emergency supply for the equipment. Check availability of power supply to consumer.
