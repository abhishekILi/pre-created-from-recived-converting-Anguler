Table No. 1
Ser Checks to be performed SAT/UNSAT
LCP- Power supply checks
1.1 At LCP, check indications available for FWD and AFT network of 220V/60Hz supply.
1.2 Set the commutator to "FWD & AFT POWER SUPPLY" and press "CONNECT". Check power supply available in one of the sockets and the same goes off when pressed "OFF"
1.3 Repeat the procedure at 1.2 for "FWD POWER SUPPLY" and check supply at one of the FWD network socket.
1.4 Repeat the procedure at 1.2 for "AFT POWER SUPPLY" and check supply at one of the AFT network socket.
Power supply checks- Sockets
1.5 FWD network-
Check following five sockets for supply for galley equipment- FG22505, FG22506, FG22508, FG22509 and FG22511
1.6 AFT network-
Check following five sockets for supply for galley equipment- FG22501, FG22502, FG22504 and FG22510
LCP- Security alarms
1.7 Press the push button of fire detection system in aft corridor. While the button is pressed, check at LCP, the 220V/60 hz supply is cut-off from the output of LCP.
1.8 Press the test button in refrigerating room for emergency horn on LCP. Check horn available.
