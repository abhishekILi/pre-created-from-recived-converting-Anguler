3.4.1 Helmet may be corded with max cord length of 6ft.
3.4.2 System should be cordless.

Stand by time - 12 hrs or more.
Talk mode - 02 hrs or more.
3.5Para 1.7 of EED 51-09At least 04 battery sets should be provided for each mobile user.
3.6Para 1.7 of EED 51-09At least 02 battery charging stations with provision to simultaneously charge batteries for up to all 16 mobile users should be supplied as part of equipment.
3.7Para 1.8 of EED 51-09Fixed. Units should have both inbuilt speakers and pluggable headsets.
3.8Para 1.4(b) of EED 51-09Reliability. The system should have full redundancy in terms of failure of Base Station and Switch.
3.9Para 2.7 of EED 51-09Lifting Arrangements. Units weighing more than 40 Kgs shall be provided with collar eyebolts or suitable lifting lugs/arrangements.
3.10Para 2.8 of EED 51-09Terminal. Bolted type terminal and crimped socket of electrolytic copper are to be provided for all incoming and outgoing cables.
3.11Para 2.9 of EED 51-09Anti-condensation Heating. Wherever necessary, equipment shall be provided with anti-condensation heating elements, which are to be switched on automatically when the equipment is switched off.
3.12Para 2.11 of EED 51-09Safety Standards. All units to be provided with earthing bolts. Each unit which is connected to ships mains are to be provided with fuse protection.
3.13Para 2.14 of EED 51-09Cabinet Cooling. Sufficient cooling and circulation of air should be provided for the system cabinets.
3.14Para 2.15 of EED 51-09Mounting Arrangement. The equipment would be mounted on shock mounts onboard ship.

4.1Para 2.1 of EED 51-09Power supply - 230V/115V, 1 phase, 50/60 Hz, System should also have provision for operating on 24V DC supply in case of emergency.
4.2Para 1.3 of EED 51-09Freq range – V/UHF 100-400 Mhz.
Channels – At least 03 channels catering for 16 wireless positions.
4.3Para 1.5(b) of EED 51-09Priorities - Priorities of positions in HDCS would be as follows: I- LSO, II- CFD, III- Bridge/Ops room, IV- Hangar. Rest of the positions can be put on the lowermost rung of hierarchy.
4.4Para 1.5(e) of EED 51-09Switching between channels is to be provided.
4.5Para 1.1 of EED 51-09System should be capable of handsfree operation on helo deck and hangar.
4.6Para 1.9 of EED 51-09Warning Indications.
4.6.1 Suitable audio, visual and vibrate warning should be available to warn user of incoming calls when on different channel.
4.6.2 Audio and visual warning for low battery should be provided.
4.7Para 1.12 of EED 51-09Recording. The system should have recording facility with the following provisions:
4.7.1 Record and playback of all Helo deck and aviation related communications.
4.7.2 Recording and replay facilities of all channels for at least 24-hrs with time tags and should be transferable to secondary storage devices like CDs and DVDs.
4.7.3 The format of recording should be of that type that can be replayed in COTS device or PCs. It should be able to synchronise the timings of helo deck CCTV system.
4.8Para 2.23 of EED 51-09BITE. The system should have capability for on line and off-line maintenance diagnostics.
4.9Para 3.17 of EED 51-09Interface. Supplier is responsible for interfacing and integration of HDCS with following ship system: 4.9.1 VCS Mk III; 4.9.2 Main broadcast; 4.9.3 Aviation broadcast.
