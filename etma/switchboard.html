4. Installation Checks
Ser Reference Checks Remarks
4.1 Pre-Power Up Checks.
EED-Q-264 (IP-55) Section 6 para (0624) 4.1.1 Switchboard area is clean, dry, no water ingress.
4.1.2 No Cables / Pipelines is Passed above switchboard.
EED-Q-261 (Illumination levels Page 31) 4.1.3 Luminosity checks of SWBD room (>150 lumens).
Section 6 & 8 Para 0630, 0631 & 0810 4.1.4 Check for any loose tools/material around.
NHQ/DEE EE/03/9712 dated 31 May 19 4.1.5 Check Material state of HV Rubber Mats (in good condition).
EED-Q-264 Section 07 Para (0725-0731) 4.1.6 Check for proper ventilation of SWBD.
4.2 Physical Condition Checks.
EED-Q-264 Section 07 Para (0725-0731) 4.2.1 Check for doors and Panels Screws and Handles.
4.2.2 Check proper operation of Hinges/Locks.
4.2.3 Check availability of Talley plates and labelling.
EED-Q-264 Section 08 & 06 Para (0812-0815) & (0653-0656) 4.2.4 Check for Cable termination (Insulation Bear/tear/damage).
EED-Q-264 Section 06 Para (0639-0643) 4.2.5 Check if bus-bar is covered with shrouds.
EED-Q-264 (IP-55) Para (0639-0643) 4.2.6 Check if MCCBs/ACBs are free to move on railing for maintenance.
EED-Q-264 Section 13 Para (1309-1310) 4.2.7 Check if healthy indication of Protection relays (if possible).
4.3 Instrumentation.
EED-Q-264 Section 09 Para (0913-0912) 4.3.1 Check operational availability of all Voltmeters.
4.3.2 Check operational availability of all Ammeters.
4.3.3 Check operational availability of all Frequency meters.
4.3.4 Check operational availability of all Power factor meters.
4.3.5 Check Synchronising panel lamps & synchroscope.
4.3.6 Check operational availability of all Meter selector switches working.
4.3.7 Check operational availability of all insulation meters.
4.4 Bus Bar Condition.
EED-Q-264 Section 06 Para (0639-0643) 4.4.1 Check Bus bar insulation >10MΩ.
4.4.2 Check Indication of LIVE/DEAD busbar on SWBD Panel.
4.5 Cable Checks.
EED-Q-264 Section 08 & 06 Para (0812-0815) & (0653-0656) 4.5.1 Check cable gland fitting. Cables should be passed via heat shrink glands.
4.5.2 Check for temperature of cables inside SWBD panel using temperature gun.
4.5.3 Check Phase sequence.
4.6 Earthing Checks. Check earthing of SWBD properly connected.
4.7 NHQ DNO letter OP/SAFETY/5106/EQPT dated 25/04/25 4.7.1 Checks if Fire Extinguisher CO2 available near switchboard.
4.7.2 Checks for the availability of Electric shock rescue Hook.
4.7.3 Checks if No flammable items is stored nearby SWBD.
