5.4.1 Section X Para 1017 No Changeover Operation
Change the 'Normal' supply voltage through Variac between 374V to 415V (within 10% of rated voltage). Check the supply should not changeover.
5.4.2.1 Changeover Operation — Normal → Alternate
Reduce the normal supply voltage through Variac below 331V (below 20% of rated voltage) / switch 'OFF' the 'Normal' supply. ACOS should changeover to 'Alternate' supply and check the O/P is through 'Alternate' supply. (Measure the changeover time)
