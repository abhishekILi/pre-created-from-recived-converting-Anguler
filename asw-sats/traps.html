General Observations
Test description

The first phase will be performed as per Navy promulgated exercise area. (i.e. near- shore operating area in low sea state) to verify all systems all functional following installation, STW, and HATs. This phase will include:

  • Launch and recovery operation at approximately 6, 9 and 12 knots.
  • Towing within the range of 6 - 12 knots.
  • Towing at a range of depths (based on available water depth)
  • Low source level transmission at a depth of 10 m or greater.

SAT1 Target test requirement (S/M Traget)

This test includes a variety of mechanical and acoustic serials. Acoustic serial will be performed against a GTI provided tgt. A brief summary of the tests are as follows:

  • Launch and recovery operations at various speeds and depths.
  • Safety demonstrations of the Winch Handling system.
  • Active and passive operations against a GTI Towed target.
  • User interface demonstrations.

SAT 2 (Submarine testing)

This test will be include active and passive operational serials against a submarines target. For the serials, it will be necessary that the submarine and TRAPS vessel sail in pre-determined geometries. This will ensure that the different features of TRAPS cab be properly tested and evaluated in a reasonable amount of time. Specific features that will be tested are:

  • Passive tracking
  • Active tracking

Note: a trained passive acoustic expert from the Indian Navy shall be available for all runs to support sonar operators in frequency recognition.
