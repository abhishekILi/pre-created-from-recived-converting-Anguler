13. Functional Checks for Device OP-10Б
Ser Description of Check Expected Results Remarks
(a) Functioning of indicators and annunciator of devices OP-10Б. After pushing the button IND CHECK on OP-10Б all indicators and annunciators of devices OP-10Б are lighted up.
(b) Check of Initial states SAMI, SEI, MSI, TGPUI. Indicators SAMI, SEI, MSI, TGPUI are lighted up on device OP-10Б.
(c) Check of instructions "+ 27V THERM ON", "+ 27V LIFT OFF", "+27V MEM", "+27V LI-LCHR", "+27V K", "+27V", A, B, C, (3Φ, 220V, 400 Hz). Indicators THERM, GPU, LI, LO, MEM, K and annunciator GPU lighted up on device OP-10Б.
(d) Check passage of signals "+27V ODZ", "+27V OBZ", "+27V HATCH CL". Indicators HATCH CL, ODZ, OBZ are lighted up on OP-10Б.
(e) Check of passage of signals "Drum1", "Drum2". Indicators "Drum1", "Drum2" are lighted up on OP-10Б.
(f) Check of instructions "+ 27V HRG", "+ 27V HRG", "+27V NRG", "+27V NRG", "+27V MSA", "+27V MSA","+27V MS", "+27V MS", "+27V AGG", "+27V AGG", "+27V UC" and signal "+27V TGPUR" Indicators HRG, HRG, NRG, NRG, MSA, MSA, MS, MS, AGG, AGG, UC, and TGPUR are lighted up on OP-10Б.
(g) Equipment Check in failure mode and EMERGENCY LAUNCH mode after lighting up the annunciator FAILURE on OP-10Б. Only indicators ISI and TGPU OFF are lighted up. The rest of indicators are off in FAILURE mode. Annunciators FAILURE, MSL SET, LCHR READY are switched on, the rest of annunciators are off. After instructions "+27V Emergency Launch" all the indicators of device OP-7И and the annunciators of device OP-10Б are off.
(h) Equipment operation Check in EMERGENCY LAUNCH if the annunciator DANGER is lighted up on device OP-10Б in LIFT-OFF mode. After instructions "+27V Emergency Launch" all the indicators of device OP-7И and the annunciators of device OP-10Б are off.
(j) Equipment operation Check in EMERGENCY LAUNCH if the annunciator DANGER is lighted up on device OP-10Б in FAILURE TO LIFT-OFF mode. All the indicators which have complied to the instructions in case of pushing the button EML on console ПКБ ОР-10 are lighted up on devices OP-7 И, OP-10Б under failure to lift off.
(k) Check of instructions "27V PAUG" and "27V RD" Indicators PAUG and RD are lighted up on device OP-10Б, it should be continued during approx. 10 Sec
(l) Equipment operation check in WT and PT modes. When toggle switch WT-PT on device OP-10Б is set to position PT and signal "TDFU" is absent the annunciator MOD READY on device OP-10Б is not lighted up. When toggle switch WT-PT on device OP-10Б is set to position WT and signal "TDFU" is absent the annunciator MOD READY on device OP-10Б continues to light up.
(m) Time 3.5 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (3.5±0.4) sec.
(n) Time 14 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (14±1.4) sec.
(p) Time 10 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (10±1) sec.
(q) Time 2.5 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (2.5±0.3) sec.
(r) Time 0.08 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (0.08±0.025) sec.
(s) Time 0.5 sec check. Counter on console ПКБ ОР-10 should indicate (0.5±0.075) sec.
(t) Time control OVER HEAT Annunciator OVERHEAT is lighted up on device OP-10Б in (10±1) Min after instruction "PD" or "+27V GPU"
14. Functional Checks for Device OP-10A
Ser Description of Check Expected Results Remarks
(a) Check of zero shift resources of summing amplifiers and base voltage resources channel comparator of device OP-10A. The value of zero shift resources of summing amplifiers and base voltage resources on each channel is measured by voltmeter connected with device OP-10A socket.
(b) Check of signal "READY" by device OP-10A and also accuracy control of instructions "Φа", "ΦН", "°Д". All indicators of device OP-10A should be lighted up in case of: Φа НаB – Φа Оtр = (±1.0 ±0.2)° ΦН НаB – ΦН Оtр = (±1.0 ±0.2)° °Д –°Д Оtр = (±64.5 ±12.9) m/s
15. Functional Checks for Device OP-7И
Ser Description of Check Expected Results Remarks
(a) Functioning check of indicators of device OP-7И. During switching on the switch key IND CHECK-OFF, all indicators of device OP-7И are lighted up.
(b) Checking continuity of circuits. Should be +10Ω in close circuit & infinite in open circuit
(c) Functioning check of monitoring circuit of phase sequence and loss with voltage 3Φ, 400Hz, 220V. If phase sequence is correct then PHA should glow. If not then it shouldn't.
(d) Check instruction "YКР". Micro-Ammeter of device OP-7И should indicate current (-10±6) μA during setting the switch key P1 to position YКР and toggle switch COARSE-FINE to COARSE.
(e) Check of instructions "+ 27V HRG", "+ 27V HRG", "+27V NRG", "+27V NRG", "+27V MSA","+27V MSA", "+27V MS","+27V MS","+27V AGG","+27V AGG", "+27V UC", and signal "+27V TGPUR". Indicators HRG, HRG, NRG, NRG, MSA, MSA, MS, MS, AGG, AGG, UC, TGPUR, G MINUS, G MINUS,M MINUS and M MINUS are lighted up on OP-7И.
(f) Equipment check in LO mode During simulation of LIFT-OFF, all indicators of device OP-7И are off.
(g) Check of instructions "27V PAUG" and "27V RD" Indicators PAUG and RD are lighted up on device OP-7И.
(h) Equipment operation check in LF (NJ) mode. Set toggle switch LF(NJ) to respective position on device OP-10Б and Micro-Ammeter on device OP-7И should indicate interruption of circuits ("0") by ΔΦа, ΔΦН (switch key P1 is in positions ΔΦа, ΔΦН and toggle switch COARSE-FINE is to position COARSE)
16. Functional Checks for Device OP-7H
Ser Description of Check Expected Results Remarks
(a) Checking continuity of circuits. Should be +10Ω in close circuit & infinite in open circuit
(b) Constant voltage 27V check Voltmeter connected with cleats ±27V of device OP-7H should indicate voltage (27.00 +2.70/-1.35)V.
(c) Functioning check of monitoring circuit of phase sequence and loss with voltage 3Φ, 400Hz, 220V. If loss of any phase in the circuit then respective phase indication should not glow on device OP-7И and OP-7H by pressing button 3Φ, 400Hz, 220V.
(d) Current control by circuits "+27V NRG", "+27V NRG","+27V HRG","+27V HRG","+27V MSA","+27V MSA", "+27V MS", "+27V MS","+27V AGG", "+27V AGG" and "+27V UC". Ammeter of device OP-7H Should indicate current (4.5±2.0) A.
